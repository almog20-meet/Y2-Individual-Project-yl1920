Seizure

Stay with the person until the seizure ends and he or she is fully awake.
Do not hold the person down or try to stop his or her movements.


1. Ease the person to the floor

2.Take a pillow, or a soft object,ike a folded jacket, and put it unser the paitent head.

3. Foam might go out from his mouth, to prevent him from choking
Turn the person gently onto one side.


After it ends, help the person sit in a safe place.






Stroke

F A S T

F - face.
Check if his both sides of the face are even
Ask him to smile, if his smile is uneven, it could be one of the signs of stroke

A- arms.
We want to check if he has the same strenght in both sides.
Ask him to put his hands straight up, in 90 drgrees to his body.
Then ask him to squeeze your hands with his hands, if he dose not press on both sides evenly,
he has one of the signs of a stroke

S - Speech
We want to check if he speaks clearly
Ask him a few questions, if he has confused or unclear speech, it could be one of the signs of stroke

T - time
Time it is the most important thing
The fast you act the more chances to save him
